# Ecosystem Grant Program (Application Process)

This module outlines the **Application Process** for the **Ecosystem Grant Program** offered by the Gurufin Foundation. This program provides funding to support the development of applications, tools, and infrastructure that expand the Gurufin Chain ecosystem.

## Key Concepts and Relationships

The application process is a core component of the broader Ecosystem Grant Program. Understanding the various stages and related concepts is crucial for prospective applicants.

*   **Ecosystem Grant Program:** This is the overarching initiative by the Gurufin Foundation to fund projects built on Gurufin Chain. Its primary goal is to foster growth and innovation within the ecosystem.
*   **Eligible Projects:** These are specific categories of projects that the Gurufin Foundation is interested in funding. They include:
    *   **DeFi:** Decentralized Finance applications like DEX integrations, lending protocols, and yield aggregators.
    *   **Infrastructure:** Foundational tools such as indexers, oracles, bridges, and developer tooling.
    *   **Applications:** User-facing applications like wallets, payment solutions, and analytics dashboards.
    *   **Education:** Resources that help expand knowledge and adoption, such as tutorials, documentation, and community resources.
*   **Application Process:** This defines the step-by-step procedure that applicants must follow to apply for a grant. It comprises the following stages:
    1.  **Submit Proposal:** The initial step where applicants detail their project, team, proposed milestones, and funding requirements.
    2.  **Review:** The stage where the Gurufin Foundation team evaluates the submitted proposal for its feasibility, technical merit, and alignment with ecosystem needs.
    3.  **Approval:** If the project is accepted after the review, it moves to this stage where funding is allocated, typically on a milestone-based schedule.
    4.  **Delivery:** The final stage where grant recipients complete their milestones and receive subsequent funding tranches upon successful completion.
*   **Contact:** Information on how to reach the Gurufin Foundation for inquiries related to the grant program. This is typically done through official channels listed on gurufin.io.

## Important Details from Source Excerpts

*   **Purpose:** The Gurufin Foundation's grant program aims to support "builders creating applications, tools, and infrastructure on Gurufin Chain" and "provides funding for projects that expand the ecosystem."
*   **Milestone-Based Funding:** Approved projects receive funding in tranches, contingent upon the successful completion of predetermined milestones.
*   **Official Contact:** All grant inquiries should be directed via the official channels listed on [gurufin.io](https://gurufin.io).
