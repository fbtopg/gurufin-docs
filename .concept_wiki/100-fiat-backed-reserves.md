# 100% Fiat-Backed Reserves

## Overview

The concept of "100% Fiat-Backed Reserves" is a foundational principle for stablecoins issued on platforms like GX Stablecoin. It guarantees that every digital token in circulation is explicitly and entirely supported by an equivalent amount of traditional fiat currency (e.g., USD, EUR, JPY) held in designated reserve accounts. This mechanism is crucial for maintaining the stablecoin's peg to its underlying fiat currency, providing stability, and fostering trust among users and regulatory bodies.

## How it Works

In practice, for every stablecoin token minted on the platform, an equivalent unit of fiat currency is deposited into a secure reserve account. Conversely, when stablecoins are redeemed, the corresponding fiat currency is released from the reserve, and the stablecoins are burned. This strict one-to-one backing model ensures that the total supply of stablecoins is always matched by the value of the assets held in reserve.
