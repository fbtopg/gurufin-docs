# 01 Overview (Compliance Framework)

This module provides a high-level overview of the Compliance Framework underpinning the GX Chain. It explains how the GX Chain is structured to adhere to regulatory requirements and ensure institutional assurance across various jurisdictions.

## What is GX Chain?

The GX Chain is a sovereign stablecoin framework designed to support fiat-backed digital currencies. It operates not as a single blockchain, but as a **network of independent Layer-1 chains**. Each of these chains issues a local sovereign stablecoin, pegged 1:1 to its respective fiat currency. This unique architecture is specifically designed to enable regulatory compliance, transparency, and operational efficiency, tailoring to each jurisdiction's distinct legal and financial frameworks.

## Key Concepts

### Compliance Framework
The overarching structure that governs the GX Chain's operations, ensuring adherence to regulatory standards. This framework is intrinsically linked to **Wallet Tiers**, where different tiers might have varying compliance requirements.

### Jurisdictional Model
The GX Chain employs a **Jurisdictional Model** where each Layer-1 chain is tailored to a specific jurisdiction's legal and financial frameworks. This model dictates which **Supported Currencies** can be issued and traded within that specific chain.

### Proof-of-Authority (PoA) Consensus
The GX Chain utilizes **Proof-of-Authority Consensus** as its underlying consensus mechanism. This type of consensus inherently facilitates strong **Governance Control**, as participating validators are pre-approved and vetted entities.

### Institutional Assurance
To provide trust and reliability, the GX Chain focuses on **Institutional Assurance**. This concept is built upon several pillars:
*   **Licensed Validators**: The network relies on a select group of approved and **Licensed Validators** who operate the Layer-1 chains. This ensures accountability and adherence to regulatory standards.
*   **Formal Audits**: Regular and **Formal Audits** are conducted to verify the integrity, security, and compliance of the GX Chain's operations.
*   **Insurance**: To further safeguard participants, **Insurance** mechanisms are in place, providing a layer of protection against unforeseen events or failures.

### Governance Control
Enabled by the Proof-of-Authority Consensus, **Governance Control** refers to the mechanisms and policies that ensure the network operates according to its established rules and regulatory obligations.

### Supported Currencies
These are the fiat-backed digital currencies issued on the GX Chain, with each currency operating within a specific **Jurisdictional Model**.

### Wallet Tiers
Different levels of user accounts or wallets within the GX Chain ecosystem, often associated with varying levels of identity verification and transaction limits, as dictated by the **Compliance Framework**.

## Relationship Summary

The **GX Chain Overview** highlights its nature as a network of **Sovereign Stablecoin Networks** built on **Jurisdiction-Specific PoA Chains**. The **Compliance Framework** is central to its design, influencing aspects like **Wallet Tiers** and how **Supported Currencies** are managed within different **Jurisdictional Models**. The adoption of **Proof-of-Authority Consensus** directly enables robust **Governance Control**. Furthermore, **Institutional Assurance** is achieved through the use of **Licensed Validators**, regular **Formal Audits**, and the provision of **Insurance**.
