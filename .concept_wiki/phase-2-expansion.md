# 03 Roadmap (Phase 2: Expansion)

This wiki page details **Phase 2: Expansion** of the Gurufin roadmap, which outlines a crucial stage in the development and growth of the Gurufin ecosystem. This phase focuses on extending the platform's capabilities, fostering ecosystem growth, and establishing critical connections for broader interoperability.

## Key Concepts and Relationships

**Phase 2: Expansion** is positioned after the foundational work completed in **Phase 1: Foundation** (which is now complete) and sets the stage for future growth outlined in **Phase 3: Scale** and ultimately **Phase 4: Global**. The activities within Phase 2 are currently in progress and are designed to broaden the operational scope and interconnectedness of the Gurufin network.

The core concepts within this phase are interconnected, working towards a more robust and expansive ecosystem:

*   **Public Testnet Launch (Season 1 Complete):** This serves as a critical testing ground for all new functionalities and ensures stability before mainnet deployment.
*   **GuruDex Oracle Priced Reserve Swap (OPRS) Architecture:** This introduces a key financial primitive for efficient and reliable reserve management within the Gurufin ecosystem.
*   **GX Stablecoin Sovereign Chain PoC with Licensed Validators:** This represents a significant step towards decentralized, regulatory-compliant stablecoin issuance. Licensed validators underscore a commitment to security and compliance.
*   **IBC Connectivity for Trust-Minimized Cosmos Ecosystem Settlement:** Integration with the Inter-Blockchain Communication (IBC) protocol is vital for seamless and trustless asset transfers and communication with other Cosmos-based chains. This directly contributes to broader interoperability.
*   **EVM Gateway for Seamless Ethereum Asset Compatibility:** This gateway bridges the Gurufin network with the vast Ethereum ecosystem, allowing for the integration and utilization of Ethereum-based assets. This expands the potential user base and asset liquidity.
*   **Price Oracle Network for FX and Benchmark Rates:** Reliable and decentralized price feeds for foreign exchange and other benchmark rates are essential for accurate valuation and stablecoin pegging, supporting financial operations within the ecosystem.
*   **Gurufin Ecosystem Development Fund for Builders:** This initiative is crucial for attracting and supporting developers, fostering the creation of new dApps and services on the Gurufin platform, directly enabling ecosystem growth.
*   **Onboarding of dApps to the Growing Network Ecosystem:** As new dApps are developed and integrated, the utility and value of the Gurufin network expand, attracting more users and driving network activity.
*   **Implementation of Governance Models and Parameter Registries:** Establishing robust governance mechanisms is vital for decentralized decision-making, allowing the community to shape the future direction and parameters of the Gurufin network.

## Important Details

*   **Status:** "In Progress." This indicates that the activities outlined in Phase 2 are actively being worked on and are not yet complete.
*   **Public Testnet (Season 1 Complete):** The completion of Season 1 of the Public Testnet signifies a successful initial testing period and progress towards full deployment.
*   **Licensed Validators for GX Stablecoin PoC:** The emphasis on "licensed validators" for the GX Stablecoin Proof-of-Concept highlights a focus on regulatory compliance and security from the outset.
*   **Interoperability Focus:** A strong emphasis is placed on interoperability through IBC connectivity for Cosmos and an EVM Gateway for Ethereum, indicating a strategy to connect Gurufin with other major blockchain ecosystems.
*   **Ecosystem Growth:** The Gurufin Ecosystem Development Fund and the onboarding of dApps directly address the expansion of the Gurufin community and its utility.
