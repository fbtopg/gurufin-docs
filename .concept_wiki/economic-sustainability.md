# 05 Tokenomics (Economic Sustainability)

The "05 Tokenomics (Economic Sustainability)" module outlines the fundamental economic model of the Gurufin ecosystem, primarily centered around its native utility token, $GXN. This section details how the token is designed to ensure the long-term viability and growth of the Gurufin Chain and GX Stablecoin networks by aligning incentives for all participants.

## Key Concepts

This module integrates several core concepts to establish a robust and sustainable economic framework:

### Tokenomics ($GXN)
The overarching concept for the Gurufin ecosystem's economic design. It defines the purpose, functions, and distribution of the $GXN token, which serves as the foundational economic layer for the Gurufin Chain and GX Stablecoin networks. The model aims to align incentives for validators, liquidity providers, governance participants, and end-users, fostering a stable and growing ecosystem.

### Token Utility
Describes the various functions and uses of the $GXN token within the Gurufin ecosystem. These utilities are crucial for driving demand, securing the network, and facilitating operations. The four core utilities for $GXN are:
*   **Staking**: Delegating GXN to validators to secure the network and earn rewards.
*   **Governance**: Participating in protocol decision-making, including voting on upgrades, parameter changes, and treasury allocation.
*   **Fee Payment**: Paying transaction fees on the network using a predictable, fiat-indexed model.
*   **Liquidity**: Providing liquidity to FX trading pools within the GuruDex OPRS trading infrastructure and earning swap fees.

### Economic Sustainability
Refers to the design principles and mechanisms that ensure the long-term health, stability, and growth of the Gurufin ecosystem. The tokenomics model, through its incentive alignment and utility design, aims to create a self-sustaining economy where all stakeholders benefit from network participation and growth.

### Staking
A critical mechanism where $GXN holders lock up their tokens to support the network's security and operations. By delegating $GXN to validators in the Delegated Proof-of-Stake (DPoS) consensus mechanism, participants contribute to network integrity and earn rewards, acting as a direct incentive for network security.

### Fee Payment
The system by which users pay for transactions and operations on the Gurufin Chain. These fees are paid in $GXN, utilizing a Guru-PEG fiat-indexed model designed to offer predictable and stable costs for on-chain activities.

### Supply & Allocation
Details the total number of $GXN tokens available and how these tokens are distributed among different stakeholders and purposes at genesis. This includes specific allocations for network rewards, ecosystem development, and other essential functions, often with vesting schedules to ensure long-term commitment.

## Important Details From Source Excerpts

*   The $GXN token is the native utility token of the Gurufin ecosystem, powering both the Gurufin Chain and GX Stablecoin networks.
*   $GXN enables four core functions: staking, governance participation, fee payments, and liquidity provisioning.
*   The tokenomics model is designed to align incentives among validators, liquidity providers, governance participants, and end-users.
