Gurufin is a specialized financial infrastructure project that combines blockchain technology with institutional-grade regulatory compliance to create a globally interoperable cross-border settlement hub. The ecosystem is built on two interconnected pillars: **Gurufin Chain**, a public Delegated Proof-of-Stake (DPoS) Layer-1 blockchain built on Cosmos SDK with Tendermint BFT consensus that serves as a neutral FX/DeFi settlement layer, and **GX Stablecoin Network**, a federation of sovereign stablecoin chains where each jurisdiction operates its own Proof-of-Authority (PoA) blockchain with licensed validators and fully 1:1 fiat-backed reserves. The project's flagship feature is **GuruDex**, an Oracle Priced Reserve Swap (OPRS) decentralized exchange purpose-built for stablecoin FX trading that eliminates slippage through oracle-verified pricing and mint/burn mechanics rather than traditional AMM liquidity pools. Key innovations include **Guru-PEG**, a fiat-indexed fee mechanism that provides predictable transaction costs, **IBC-first interoperability** for atomic cross-chain settlement, a **Live Reserve Scanner** for real-time proof-of-reserves verification, and embedded compliance hooks that align with FATF standards and jurisdictional requirements. The platform is designed to bridge traditional finance and Web3 by delivering FMI-grade reliability, deterministic finality, transparent observability for regulators, and sub-second settlement for retail and institutional users alike.
