# Vision & Mission

Gurufin was founded with a clear mission: to solve the fundamental inefficiencies in global finance that have persisted for decades. Despite rapid technological advancement, the movement of money across borders remains slow, expensive, and inaccessible to billions of people.

## The Problem: A Broken Global Financial System

The current global financial infrastructure suffers from critical pain points that affect businesses, institutions, and individuals worldwide.

### High Costs of Cross-Border Transactions

International money transfers remain prohibitively expensive. The average cost of sending remittances globally hovers around 6-7%, with some corridors exceeding 10%. For businesses, these costs compound into billions of dollars lost annually to intermediary fees, currency conversion spreads, and correspondent banking charges.

### Slow Settlement Times

Traditional cross-border payments take 2-5 business days to settle. In an era of instant digital communication, waiting days for money to move is an unacceptable bottleneck for global commerce. This delay creates cash flow challenges, increases counterparty risk, and limits the velocity of economic activity.

### Limited Access and Financial Exclusion

Over 1.4 billion adults worldwide remain unbanked, with billions more underbanked. Traditional financial rails require extensive infrastructure, regulatory relationships, and capital that exclude emerging markets and underserved populations from participating in the global economy.

### Fragmented Liquidity

The foreign exchange market, despite being the largest financial market in the world with over $7 trillion in daily volume, operates through fragmented, opaque systems. Liquidity is siloed across institutions, creating inefficiencies in price discovery and execution.

### Regulatory Complexity

Navigating the patchwork of global financial regulations creates significant compliance burdens. Businesses must establish relationships with multiple licensed entities across jurisdictions, each with different requirements and standards.

## Our Vision: A Unified Global Settlement Layer

Gurufin envisions a world where value moves as freely and instantly as information. We are building the infrastructure for a truly global financial system that is:

| Attribute | Description |

## Our Mission: Building the Stablecoin FX Layer

Our mission is to create the definitive infrastructure layer for stablecoin-based foreign exchange and cross-border payments. We achieve this through:

### Sovereign Stablecoin Network

A network of jurisdiction-specific stablecoins (GX Stablecoins) that are fully backed by local fiat reserves and operated by licensed validators. This ensures regulatory compliance while maintaining the efficiency benefits of blockchain technology.

### Hybrid Execution Model

A unique trading infrastructure that combines the accessibility of automated market makers (AMM) for retail users with the precision of request-for-quote (RFQ) systems for institutional participants, ensuring optimal execution for all users.

### Atomic Cross-Chain Settlement

Payment-versus-Payment (PvP) settlement that eliminates counterparty risk in cross-border transactions by ensuring simultaneous, atomic exchange of assets across different chains.
